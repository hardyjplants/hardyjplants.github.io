Organic

All of our plants are, unless otherwise marked, available organic. This means that everything from the soil mix we use, to the fertilizers, pest control, and insect control methods used comply with the organic standards set by the USDA. Even plants not marked as organic have never had any chemical insecticides or pesticides applied.

  • Organic FAQs (Organic.org)*
  • Top 10 Reasons to Support Organic (Organic.org)*
  • Organic Myths (Organic.org)*

Non-GMO

Whether organic or not, every plant we sell is non-GMO. GMOs, or genetically modified organisms, are organisms that have undergone genetic engineering. You can rest assured knowing that the fruit you consume from a plant purchased from Hardy J Plants is not the product of a genetically engineered plant (and hey, you even grew it yourself).

  • What is GMO? (nongmoproject.org)*
  • What is Non-GMO? (non-gmoreport.com)*
  • GMO Facts (nongmoproject.org)*

Georgia Grown

While we try our best to grow everything we sell, sometimes we must purchase plants from another distributor. Plants being locally grown is important to us. That's why we make sure that even plants not grown by us are grown right here in Georgia.
